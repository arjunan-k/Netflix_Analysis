Netflix

Netflix is an American subscription streaming service and production company based in Los Gatos, California that keeps growing bigger and better with its popularity, shows and content. Netflix was founded on August 29, 1997 by Reed Hastings and Marc Randolph. It offers a film and television series library through distribution deals as well as its own productions, known as Netflix Originals.
Today we are going to explore what's inside the Netflix.

Content Release Per Month Over the Years

If a producer wants to release some TV Show content, Considering the year 2021, January, March and May was the months when comparatively much less content was released. Therefore, these months may be a good choice for the success of a new release of TV Shows.

Types of Ratings in Movies

  • The largest count of movies is made by the 'TV-MA' rating, which is designed for mature audiences only.
  • Second largest is the 'TV-14' rating, which may be inappropriate for children younger than 14 years of age.
  • Third largest is the very popular 'R' rating, which has been assessed as having material which may be unsuitable for children under the age of 17.
  • In conclusion Netflix focuses more on the adult & mature audience.

Movies v/s TV Shows
